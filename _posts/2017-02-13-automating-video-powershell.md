Linking today to some great content from another Ian (ProTip: get to know an Ian, we're full of useful knowledge). Ian Morrish posts about automating a variety of methods of [automating A/V equipment using PowerShell](https://ianmorrish.wordpress.com). Lots of useful stuff in here.

No Windows? No worries, you can install [PowerShell on MacOS and Linux](http://www.howtogeek.com/267858/how-to-install-microsoft-powershell-on-linux-or-os-x/) too.

I've put some feelers out to some of my streaming equipment vendors to find out what kind of automation hooks and APIs they support.

Meanwhile, [Wowza](http://www.wowza.com) has a [REST API for both its Streaming Engine and Cloud products](https://www.wowza.com/products/capabilities/api-development). Integrating this into PowerShell should be relatively straightforward. Any PowerShell wizards wanna take a stab at it?

Stay tuned.
