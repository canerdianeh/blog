I've received a few comments and e-mails to my post about the [Wowza Stream Class](http://nerdian.ca/wowza-stream-class/ "Using the Wowza Stream Class") asking if I had any kind of playlist generator. I have something in Excel that I use for our weekly live stream and scheduled rebroadcasts. It may require heavy modification to suit your purposes, but it's helpful to really see how a playlist can come together.

The sheet makes heavy use of some utterly absurd [CONCATENATE](http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/excel-help/concatenate-HP005209020.aspx "Excel CONCATENATE function") functions.

Download : [WowzaPlaylistGenerator.xlsx](http://ianbeyer-blogassets.s3.amazonaws.com/WowzaPlaylistGenerator.xlsx "Wowza Stream Class Playlist Generator")
