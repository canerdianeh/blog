Thanks to [@RaamDev](http://twitter.com/raamdev "@RaamDev") for his handy little bit of [code for doing HMAC signatures](http://raamdev.com/2008/amazon-s3-hmac-signatures-without-pear-or-php5/ "S3 HMAC Signatures without PEAR"). Makes use of the [AWS SDK for PHP](http://aws.amazon.com/sdkforphp/ "Amazon Web Services SDK for PHP") to list the buckets.
